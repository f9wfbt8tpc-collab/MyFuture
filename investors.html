/01

Adaptive aptitude engine

A 20-minute multi-modal assessment — culturally calibrated for African contexts — that maps cognitive strengths, interests, and constraints into a learner's unique signal.

/02

Pathway recommender

An AI agent that ranks degrees, apprenticeships, and skills tracks against the learner's signal — weighted for affordability, employability, and visa or geographic constraints.

/03

Bursary & opportunity matching

Automatically surfaces funding, scholarships, and apprenticeship slots the learner actually qualifies for — and pre-fills the application.

/04

School & employer dashboards

Counsellors, governments, and employers see anonymised cohort-level signal: where the talent is, what it needs, and where to invest.

04 — Business model

Free for learners. Paid by the institutions who need them.

Schools & districts

SaaS · per learner / yr

Counsellor dashboards, cohort analytics, and matric-readiness reports. Sold to private school groups and ministries of education.

Universities & funders

Placement & CPA

Qualified, pre-screened applicants delivered to partner institutions and bursary funds — paid on enrolment and on placement.

Employers

Talent pipeline · subscription

Banks, telcos, and mining groups subscribe to match emerging talent into apprenticeships and graduate programmes — solving early-career hiring at the source.

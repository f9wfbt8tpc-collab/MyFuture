05 — Common questions

The things everyone asks first.

Is the assessment really free for students?

Yes — completely. The assessment, the match report, the bursary surfacing, and the application tracking are free for every learner, on any device, in any of our supported languages. We're paid by institutions and funders who get value from the network — never by the learners themselves.

How long does the assessment take?

About 20 minutes if you do it in one sitting. You can pause and pick up later from any device. We've designed it to work well on a low-end smartphone with intermittent data — a single completed assessment uses less than 5MB.

What languages is MyFuture available in?

Currently English, French, Swahili, and isiZulu. We're adding Yoruba, Hausa, Amharic, and Portuguese over the next two cohorts. The assessment is culturally calibrated per region — not just translated.

How do you handle children's data and privacy?

MyFuture is built POPIA-first, with parental consent flows for under-18 learners and strict data-minimisation throughout. Learner data is never sold. Aggregated, anonymised cohort-level signal is shared with institutional partners only — and only with explicit consent for any 1:1 outreach.

How accurate are the recommendations?

Our recommender draws on validated psychometric research, real outcome data from our pilot cohort, and the published requirements of partner institutions. The result is a fit score with explicit confidence bands — not a black-box answer. Every learner can see why a path was recommended, and adjust the inputs.

How do universities and sponsors pay?

Universities pay on enrolment for matched applicants, plus an optional dashboard subscription. Bursary sponsors pay either a per-applicant filtering fee or an annual partnership fee for white-label deployments. Schools pay a low per-learner annual licence. Pricing is transparent and tiered for the public sector.

What if I'm not happy with my recommendations?

You can re-take the assessment any time, or refine specific inputs (your subjects, your constraints, what's important to you) without starting over. You can also book a free 20-min call with a real counsellor through the app — they'll walk through your report with you.

How we use your information

We use your personal information to:

  • Generate your aptitude assessment results and pathway recommendations
  • Surface bursaries, scholarships, and apprenticeships you may qualify for
  • Pre-fill applications you choose to submit through MyFuture
  • Connect you with a counsellor when you request one
  • Send you transactional notifications (e.g. application status updates)
  • Send you optional marketing communications, only if you have opted in separately
  • Improve our recommender models, using anonymised and aggregated data only
  • Detect fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with our legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. Ever. This is a hard line, not a marketing claim.

06

Lawful basis for processing

Under POPIA and equivalent regulations, we only process your personal information when we have a lawful basis to do so:

  • Consent — for assessments, marketing communications, and any sharing with third parties
  • Performance of a contract — to deliver the service you have signed up for
  • Legitimate interest — for fraud prevention, security, and product improvement (always balanced against your rights)
  • Legal obligation — where the law requires us to retain or disclose information
  • Vital interest — only in rare cases where someone's safety is at immediate risk

You can withdraw your consent at any time by emailing privacy@myfuture.africa. Withdrawal does not affect any processing we did before you withdrew.

07

Children & minors

Most of our users are under 18. We treat their information with extra care.

Parental consent

Anyone under 18 must have a parent or legal guardian provide verifiable consent before we process their personal information for anything beyond browsing the public website. We verify consent through one or more of:

  • Email confirmation from the parent's email address, with a unique verification link
  • SMS or WhatsApp verification of the parent's mobile number
  • For school-deployed installations, the school confirming bulk parental consent under their existing learner consent framework

What we restrict for minors

  • No marketing communications, in any form, to anyone under 18
  • No profiling for advertising purposes
  • No sharing of any minor's identifiable information with universities or sponsors without explicit parental consent for that specific share
  • Stricter data minimisation — we collect only the fields strictly required to deliver the service

Special protections

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided personal information to us without your consent, please contact us at privacy@myfuture.africa. We will delete the information promptly and take reasonable steps to ensure it is not retained.

This is the most legally sensitive section. POPIA section 35 has specific rules on processing children's information; Nigeria's NDPR and Kenya's DPA each have their own. Confirm exact wording with a lawyer in each jurisdiction. Document your verifiable consent flow before going live.

Security measures

We protect your personal information using:

  • Encryption in transit — all connections use TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Encryption at rest — data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 or equivalent
  • Access controls — staff only access personal information on a need-to-know basis, with audit logging
  • Multi-factor authentication — required for all staff accounts and all sensitive systems
  • Regular security review — including vulnerability scanning and periodic penetration testing
  • Secure development practices — code review, dependency scanning, secret management
  • Data minimisation — we collect the least data needed to deliver the service

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@myfuture.africa.

Automated decisions & AI

Our pathway recommender uses machine learning models to suggest study and career paths that may suit you. These suggestions are recommendations, not decisions — you are always in control of which path to pursue.

How our AI works

  • The recommender uses your assessment responses, stated preferences, and the published requirements of partner institutions
  • It produces a fit score with an explicit confidence band — never a black-box answer
  • You can see, in plain language, why each path was recommended
  • You can adjust your inputs and re-run the recommendation any time

Your rights regarding AI

  • Explanation — request a plain-language explanation of any specific recommendation
  • Human review — speak to a human counsellor if you disagree with a recommendation
  • Opt-out — choose not to receive automated recommendations and use the platform manually instead

Our AI governance framework is aligned to the NIST AI Risk Management Framework, with additional child-safety controls. We document, test, and monitor every model that affects user-facing recommendations.

Breach notification

If we discover a breach of personal information that creates a real risk of harm to you, we will:
